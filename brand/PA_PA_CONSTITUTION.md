# PA PA Brand Constitution

Version 1.0

This document defines the principles that guide every decision made for PA PA.

The website may change.
Campaigns may change.
Products may change.

These principles should not.

---

# Purpose

PA PA exists to create beautifully made drinks that quietly become part of life's best moments.

We believe the best drinks don't dominate the occasion.

They become part of it.

---

# What PA PA Is

PA PA is the bottle that's there for the stories, the laughter and the memories.

It represents consistency, confidence and warmth.

It never asks to be the centre of attention.

Yet somehow it always belongs at the table.

---

# What We Believe

People matter more than products.

Conversation matters more than critique.

The occasion matters more than the bottle.

Good wine should be approachable.

Quality should never feel intimidating.

Consistency builds trust.

Trust creates loyalty.

---

# Product Philosophy

Every PA PA product should be:

• Beautifully made.
• Easy to love.
• Easy to drink.

It should satisfy the wine enthusiast without intimidating everyone else.

People should simply say:

"Yum."

Then pour another glass.

---

# Our Position

We are not luxury.

We are not cheap.

We are the bottle people confidently buy again.

The bottle they're proud to pour.

The bottle they're always happy to open.

---

# Tone of Voice

Warm.

Confident.

Quiet.

Never arrogant.

Never pretentious.

Never apologetic.

We write like a trusted friend who knows good wine.

Not like a wine critic.

---

# We Avoid

Pretension.

Wine jargon.

Trying too hard.

Shouting.

Complexity for its own sake.

Awards as marketing.

Fear of simplicity.

---

# We Embrace

People.

Stories.

Hospitality.

Generosity.

Consistency.

Balance.

Joy.

---

# Campaign Filter

Before launching any campaign, ask:

Does this feel like PA PA?

Would someone who loves PA PA recognise this as us?

Does this strengthen the brand?

Or simply advertise a product?

If the answer is no, don't do it.

---

# The Table Test

If this idea makes the people around the table more important than the bottle...

it's probably a PA PA idea.

If it makes the bottle more important than the people...

it probably isn't.

---

# Our Promise

Every bottle carrying the PA PA name should earn its place at the table.

Beautifully made.

Easy to love.

Easy to drink.
