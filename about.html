About PA PA

Australian wine made for everyday tables.

PA PA is an Australian drinks brand creating fresh, balanced and approachable wines for everyday enjoyment. Beginning with Pinot Noir and Pinot Gris, PA PA combines quality fruit and thoughtful winemaking with a simple belief: good wine should be easy to choose, easy to enjoy and welcome around the whole table.

PA PA wines are made for shared meals, unexpected guests, long lunches and ordinary evenings that become lasting memories. They are designed to offer real quality without pretension, with styles that appeal to experienced wine drinkers as well as people who simply know what they enjoy.

Wine is the first expression of PA PA, but the brand is not defined by one beverage. Every future PA PA product must be beautifully made, dependable and worthy of a place at the centre of the table.

Easy to drink. Easy to love.
